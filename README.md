# 🚀 SkillLaunchAI

SkillLaunchAI is a user-intuitive, AI-integrated career coach designed for college students.  
It helps students prepare for the workforce by generating personalized career roadmaps, simulating HR interviews, and recommending opportunities based on skills and career goals.

This project was built as a prototype during the AI for Atmanirbhar Bharat: HEI Pre-Summit Hackathon (2026) at Assam down town University, where Team Reload won 2nd Prize 🏆.

---

## 🎯 Features

### Personalized Career Roadmaps
Generate AI-powered career plans tailored to the user’s:
- skills
- experience level
- career goals
- available study time

### HR Interview Simulator
Practice interview questions with an AI interviewer that:
- asks realistic HR questions
- analyzes your responses
- provides feedback

### Weakness Tracker
Detects common interview weaknesses such as:
- poor communication
- weak fundamentals
- lack of examples

### Opportunity Recommendations
Suggests:
- job roles
- internships
- platforms to apply on
- relevant certifications

### Skill-Based Learning Plans
Creates structured learning plans based on:
- current skill level
- career target
- time available for study
